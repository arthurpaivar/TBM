---
description: Evaluate a business case proposal for technology investment
allowed-tools: Read, Write, Edit, Grep, Glob, Bash, WebSearch, WebFetch, Task
argument-hint: [business case document or investment proposal to evaluate]
---

Evaluate the business case proposal in $ARGUMENTS using the tech-bm-specialist and it-portfolio-management skills.

Follow this process:
1. Load the tech-bm-specialist and it-portfolio-management skills from this plugin
2. Review the business case structure and completeness
3. Validate financial assumptions (NPV, IRR, payback period, TCO)
4. Assess strategic alignment with technology portfolio
5. Evaluate risk factors and mitigation strategies
6. Compare against industry benchmarks where applicable
7. Provide a scored assessment with recommendations

Output: Business Case Evaluation with Financial Assessment, Strategic Alignment Score, Risk Analysis, Benchmark Comparison, and Go/No-Go Recommendation.
